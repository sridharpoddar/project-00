Instructions

  1. Pick your side- toirtose on the left and hare on the right!
  2. The first one to reach the middle line wins!
  3. The tortoise can be controlled with the caps lock key
  4. The hare can be controlled with the return key
  5. On the count of 3, press your respective keys to get started!
  6. The first person to press the key will probably win!
